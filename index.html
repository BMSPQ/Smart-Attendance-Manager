Smart Attendance Manager

Smart Attendance Manager is a simple yet powerful tool designed to help students manage their attendance efficiently. It provides real-time insights into their attendance status and helps them make informed decisions about attending or skipping classes.

Key Features:

  • Attendance Goal Calculator: Find out how many more classes you need to attend to meet your attendance target.
  • Bunk Planner: Calculate how many classes you can safely miss without falling below the required attendance threshold.
  • Interactive Interface: User-friendly input fields for total classes conducted, attended, and desired attendance percentage.
  • Quick Results: Instant calculations to help students plan their schedule effectively.

Why Use This Tool?

Stay informed about your attendance status.

Avoid falling short of minimum attendance requirements.

Plan your academic and personal time better with clear insights.
